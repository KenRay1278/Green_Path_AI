About GreenPath AI

GreenPath AI helps compare route choices by showing how a time-first path differs from a lower-emission alternative on an interactive road map.

How Route Comparison Works

The app uses A* search to evaluate paths between two selected points. One route prioritizes estimated travel time, while the other uses a simplified particulate-emission cost so the tradeoff is easier to see.

What The Estimates Mean

PM2.5 and PM10 values are simplified emitted-mass estimates for route comparison. They are not official air-quality readings and do not model wind, traffic volume, weather, or street-level dispersion.

Designed For Clarity

Route labels, animation, sound feedback, and visual contrast are used to make the search process understandable without turning the map into a technical debugging screen.
